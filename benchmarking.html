Puppet Usability Benchmarking

The Challenge

Over the summer I worked as a UX intern at Puppet. Puppet is a company makes IT automation software for managing and securing your infrastructure.

My main project was to develop and measure usability benchmarks for Puppet products. In order to accomplish this, the benchmarks needed to be tested with users in order to measure the current performance of the product.

Goals

  • Research and develop a methodology for measuring the overall ease of use of Puppet Enterprise.
  • Measure the current usability of Puppet Enterprise to set a quantitative baseline for tracking the performance of each release.
  • Discover areas for improvement and specific usability issues through user testing.

What is usability benchmarking?

Usability benchmarking is a method of measuring the overall ease of use of your product to evaluate the performance of future releases. A benchmarking study doesn’t care about the why like in a heuristic evaluation; it’s a raw measure of user performance.
