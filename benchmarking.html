Puppet Usability Benchmarking

The Challenge

The goal of this study was to test and iterate on a set of benchmarks which can be used to measure the overall usability of Puppet Enterprise to set a baseline for future releases.

My Role

Create usability metrics, design high-level tasks that cover a wide range of functionality and facilitate benchmarking test sessions with live users to gather data.

What is usability benchmarking?

Usability benchmarking is a method of measuring the overall ease of use of your product to evaluate the performance of future releases. A benchmarking study doesn’t care about the why like in a heuristic evaluation; it’s a raw measure of user performance.
